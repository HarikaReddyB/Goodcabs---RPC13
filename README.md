# Goodcabs

**About Company:** 

➜ The company is mainly focused on tier-2 cities.

➜ Goodcabs Vision is to support the local drivers to help them make a sustainable living in their hometowns while ensuring excellent service to passengers.

# Problem Statement:
Goodcabs operates in 10 tier-2 cities across India, and has set ambitious performance targets for 2024 to drive growth and improve passenger satisfaction. 

As part of this initiative, the Goodcabs management team aims to assess the company’s performance across key metrics, including Trip volume, Passenger satisfaction, Repeat passenger rate, Trip distribution, and the balance between new and repeat passengers.

# Key Metrics:

- Total Revenue : 108M
- Total Trips : 426K
- Avg Fare per Trip : ₹ 254.02
- Avg Fare per KM : ₹ 13.28
- Total Distance Travelled : 8M
- Avg Trip Distance : 19.13
- Total New Trips : 177K
- Total Repeated Trips : 249K
- Total Passengers : 238K
- Total New Passengers : 177K
- Total Repeated Passengers : 61K
- Repeat Passenger Rate % : 25.73%
- Average Passenger Rating : 7.66

# Insights:
- Jaipur has the highest trip volume & Mysore has recorded with lowest trips.
- The average Driver rating is consistent over 6 months and varies slightly b/w 7.9 - 7.8.
- The average Passenger rating is high in January, and low in May & June.
- Trip Demand: Weekend - Jaipur & Kochi. Weekday: Lucknow & Surat.
- Jaipur has the highest revenue and Coimbatore has recorded the lowest revenue.
- Revenue peaks in February & March, and Low in June.
- High Demand months are: February & March. Low Demand Months are: January & June.
- Visakhapatnam & Jaipur has the highest Repeat Passenger Rate % frequency (i.e. 2-trips).
- Repeat Passenger Rate % - 25.73%.
- Surat & Lucknow have the highest RPR%. Jaipur & Mysore have the lowest RPR%.
- May has the highest RPR% & January has the lowest RPR%.


# Recommendations: 
- Focus on expanding operations in high-performing cities like Jaipur to maximize growth opportunities.
- Promote campaigns and marketing strategies in underperforming cities.
- Introduce emerging trends like EVs to reduce fuel usage & maintenance costs and promote environmental sustainability.
- Launch promotions during low-demand months to boost the number of passengers.
- Collaborate with local businesses, hotels, malls, & event venues to boost the demand and increase the brand visibility.
- Prioritize the feedback collection to improve service quality & pricing to attract more passengers.
